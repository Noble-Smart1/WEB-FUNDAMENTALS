Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is CSylvia Naturals?

CSylvia Naturals is a brand committed to crafting premium organic hair creams and skincare products. Our formulations are created using the finest organic ingredients, free from harsh chemicals, ensuring a natural and nourishing experience for your hair and skin.

Are CSylvia Naturals products suitable for all hair types?

Yes, our products are designed to cater to a diverse range of hair types and textures. Whether you have curly, straight, wavy, or coily hair, CSylvia Naturals has products to enhance the natural beauty of your hair.

Are CSylvia Naturals products cruelty-free?

Absolutely! CSylvia Naturals is proud to be a cruelty-free brand. We do not test our products on animals, and we are committed to promoting ethical practices in the beauty industry.

Product Information

How do I use CSylvia Naturals organic hair creams?

For our organic hair creams, take a small amount and apply it evenly through damp or dry hair. Style as usual. Detailed instructions can be found on each product page.

Can I use CSylvia Naturals skincare products for sensitive skin?

Yes, our skincare products are formulated with gentle, organic ingredients suitable for various skin types, including sensitive skin. However, it's always a good idea to do a patch test before applying new products to your face.

Are CSylvia Naturals products safe for pregnant women?

Our products are made with natural and organic ingredients. While most pregnant women can use them without any issues, it's advisable to consult with your healthcare provider before introducing new skincare products during pregnancy.

Ordering and Shipping

Where can I purchase CSylvia Naturals products?

You can conveniently purchase CSylvia Naturals products through our official website. Visit our online store to explore our range of organic hair creams and skincare products.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit cards and secure online payment options. You can find the complete list of accepted payment methods during the checkout process.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Use this tracking number on our website to monitor the status and location of your shipment.
